1. IoT (Internet of Things)

HomeWise utilizes IoT technology to connect your home's appliances, devices, and sensors, enabling seamless automaion, remote control, and real-time monitoring.

2. ML (Machine Learning)

HomeWise employs ML algorithms to personalize your smart home experience, optimizing energy consumption, predicitng maintenance needs, and adapting to your preferences over time.

3. NLP (Natural Language Processing)

HomeWise integrates NLP tech for advanced voice control, allowing you to interact with your smart home using natural language commands. Simply speak your commands, and HomeWise will respond accordingly.

4. Sensor Fusion

Sensor fusion combines data from miltiple sensors to create a more comprehensive and accurate understanding of the envirenment. HomeWise utilizes sensor fusion enhance security and environmental monitoring
